About RecipeMagic

How it Works

Using the power of generative AI from Google, RecipeMagic allows you to search for recipes by food name or ingredients. Here's how:

  • Search by Food Name: Simply enter the name of the dish you're craving, and RecipeMagic will find the perfect recipes for you.
  • Search by Ingredients: Have some ingredients on hand but not sure what to make? Enter them into RecipeMagic, and we'll suggest creative recipes based on what you have.

Technology

RecipeMagic leverages cutting-edge technologies to deliver a seamless experience:

  • Generative AI: Our AI-powered system, developed with Google's technology, analyzes millions of recipes to provide you with personalized recommendations.
  • HTML5 & CSS3: Modern web standards ensure a smooth and responsive user interface.
  • Bootstrap: We use Bootstrap for our frontend design, making RecipeMagic accessible on any device.
